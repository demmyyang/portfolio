PROJECT DESCRIPTION: A fashion production app that delves into the problem space of sourcing materials and services

BACKGROUND: The New York City garment district spans just a few blocks but is home to hundreds if not thousands of businesses. Even with so many options, many find it difficult to break into and filter out the right partners and sources for fabrics and materials.

GOALS: A finished front-end of a mobile app platform app for both customers & businesses(factories and material stores) presentable to the investors:
- to understand how the platform app works
- for customers who need sourcing info and access to service & material providers
- and for businesses who want to register their businesses and reach out to more clients & customers

TOOLS USED: Figma, Miro, Trello, Zoom, Google Forms, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
